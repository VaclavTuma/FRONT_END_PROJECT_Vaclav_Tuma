Explore the heart of Europe!

Czech Railways (ČD) offers a comfortable and efficient way to travel across the Czech Republic and Central Europe, connecting major cities and beautiful landscapes. With modern trains and easy access to neighboring countries, ČD is the perfect choice for exploring the heart of Europe.

What types of trains can you see after arriving to Czech Republic?

SC Pendolino

The fastest SuperCity train connections on the Ostrava Olomouc Pardubice Prague Pilsen Cheb route offer very comfortable travel in 1st or 2nd carriage class with required reservations.

Routes:

Prague - Olomouc - Ostrava

Cheb - Pilsen - Prague - Ostrava

Prague - Ostrava - Poprad - Košice

RailJet

A fast and comfortable travel between Brno and Prague, and a direct connection to Vienna and Graz in Austria. These trains offer three carriage classes – 2nd class (Economy Class), 1st class (First Class) and Business Class for the greatest comfort.

Routes:

Prague - Brno - Vienna - Graz

Prague - Dresden - Berlin

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS - FAQ

Where can I go?

Our trains are going to every major city, town and village in Czech Republic. Moreover you can try our intenational services.

Does ČD provide international services?

Yes! You can go with as to all surounding countries. Germany, Poland, Slovakia, Austria, Hungary and Switzerland

Can I buy seat reservation?

Yes! Seat reservation is for free if you buy ticket on České Dráhy webside or in mobile application Můj Vlak. It is also possible to buy seat reservation separatly for InterRail travelers.
