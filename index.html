Czech Railways (ČD) offers a comfortable and efficient way to travel across the Czech Republic and Central Europe, connecting major cities and beautiful landscapes. With modern trains and easy access to neighboring countries, ČD is the perfect choice for exploring the heart of Europe.

What types of trains are commonly encountered upon arriving in the Czech Republic?

SC Pendolino

❮ ❯

The fastest SuperCity train connections on the Ostrava Olomouc Pardubice Prague Pilsen Cheb route offer very comfortable travel in 1st or 2nd carriage class with required reservations.

Routes:

Prague - Olomouc - Ostrava

Cheb - Pilsen - Prague - Ostrava

Prague - Ostrava - Poprad - Košice

RailJet

❮ ❯

A fast and comfortable travel between Brno and Prague, and a direct connection to Vienna and Graz in Austria. These trains offer three carriage classes – 2nd class (Economy Class), 1st class (First Class) and Business Class for the greatest comfort.

Routes:

Prague - Brno - Vienna - Graz

Prague - Dresden - Berlin

InterJet

❮ ❯

Fast and comfortable travel between Prague and Western Bohemia with modern trains.

Routes:

Prague - Pilsen - Cheb

Prague - Ústí nad Labem - Cheb

Prague - Ostrava - Žilina

ComfortJet

❮ ❯

ComfortJet: the rising star of high-speed rail travel, setting new standards with top-tier comfort and cutting-edge technology. Experience journeys at up to 230 km/h with plush seating, seamless connectivity, and a host of premium amenities for a ride that redefines excellence.

Routes:

Prague - Dresden - Berlin

Prague - Ostrava - Poprad - Košice

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS - FAQ

Where can I go?

Our trains connect every major city, town, and village across the Czech Republic. Additionally, you can explore our international services.

Does ČD provide international services?

Absolutely! Travel with us to all neighboring countries. Germany, Poland, Slovakia, Austria, Hungary, and even Switzerland are ready to welcome you!

Can I buy seat reservation?

Yes! Seat reservations are free when you purchase your ticket through the České dráhy website or the Můj Vlak mobile app. InterRail travelers can also purchase seat reservations separately if needed.
